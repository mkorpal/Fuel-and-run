⚠️ RED-S Awareness

Relative Energy Deficiency in Sport (RED-S) can occur in high-volume runners on a calorie deficit. Watch for: persistent fatigue, poor recovery, disrupted sleep, mood changes, or stress fractures. If you notice these, increase fuelling and consult a sports dietitian or GP.
